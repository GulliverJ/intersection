Smart Intersection

v0.1

In an environment where vehicles are fully autonomous, the inefficiencies that arise from existing traffic flow control measures (such as traffic lights) can be mitigated entirely. A smart intersection enables seamless coordination of incoming traffic, such that throughput can be maximised whilst avoiding any risk of collision. The intersection controller is responsible for scheduling and monitoring vehicles as they pass through, whilst the vehicles themselves are responsible only for driving as instructed.

This prototype implementation uses timestamp-based locking, assigning to each incoming vehicle a time of arrival that allows for safe passage. Vehicles generated with a randomly chosen path and are then dynamically scheduled in real-time. Later versions will offer controls to enable a user to influence the speed and traffic flow.
